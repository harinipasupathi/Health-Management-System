# Health-Management-System
Key Features of the Health Management System
Patient Management:
Registration and management of patient details.
View and update patient history.
Search functionality for quick access to patient records.
Appointment Scheduling:
Schedule, reschedule, or cancel appointments.
Calendar view for doctors to manage their schedules.
Notifications and reminders for patients and doctors.
Billing and Payments:
Generate invoices for consultations, treatments, and services.
Track payments and outstanding balances.
Integration with payment gateways for online payments.
Medical Records Management:
Store and manage patient medical records, including prescriptions, lab results, and treatment history.
Secure access to records with role-based permissions.
Doctor and Staff Management:
Manage profiles and schedules of doctors, nurses, and other staff.
Role-based access for different users (admin, doctor, nurse, receptionist).
Reports and Analytics:
Generate reports on appointments, revenue, patient demographics, etc.
Dashboard for visualizing key metrics and analytics.
User Authentication and Authorization:
Secure login for different user roles.
Manage permissions for accessing different modules of the application.
